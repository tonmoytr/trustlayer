Key Facts About TSD

VC for Decentralised Scientific Research — but governed by the people

We deserve deeper, more personal engagement with our health, one that enhances both our personal well-being and advances the future of medical research.

TSD aims to decentralise and secure the world's health data, transforming it into a valuable resource that benefits both individuals and the broader scientific community, making scientific research more accessible, transparent, and collaborative.

TSD started back in 2021 as a telemedicine startup, building mobile solutions for hospitals and clinical research facilities in the U.S. We have now developed into a decentralised,community - governed organization advancing health innovation though open a science and secure data systems ensuring we are primed to be in a position to be at the forefront in the ever growing cryptosphere
